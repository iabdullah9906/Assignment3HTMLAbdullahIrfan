My story

I grew up in Calgary, Alberta and have stayed here for school and work. Technology first caught my attention through gaming, then through building and upgrading computers.

Over time I moved from just using computers to wanting to understand how they work and how to fix them. That interest led me to the Computer Information Systems program at Mount Royal University.

How I work

I like clear steps, hands-on work, and direct feedback. I enjoy breaking a problem into smaller pieces and testing different solutions until it works.

What I value

Reliability, effort, and communication matter to me. I try to show up prepared, finish what I start, and own my mistakes both in work and on the field.

Short-term goals

Build stronger foundations in programming, web development, and IT support. Gain part-time or internship experience in a technical role while I study.

Long-term goals

Work in a role where I support users, solve technical issues, and keep learning. I would also like to stay connected to cars and soccer in some way if I can.
